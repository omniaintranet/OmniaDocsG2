Omnia Feed app
==============

The Omnia Feed app is available to download at `App store <https://apps.apple.com/se/app/omnia-feed/id1185841164>`_ and `Google Play <https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=se.precio.news>`_.

Supported iOS versions
----------------------

The Omnia Feed app supports the current major version of iOS and the three preceding major versions. Devices running iOS versions older than these are not supported.

For example, if the current version is iOS X, the app supports iOS X, X-1, X-2, and X-3.

.. note::

   This support policy is partly due to the app’s reliance on external authentication solutions, including Microsoft Authenticator and BankID. These applications maintain similar support policies for iOS versions, which may limit compatibility with older operating system versions.
